## ‚úÖ Coverbase Solution Overview
Coverbase centralizes and automates the full vendor lifecycle:

### **Automated Vendor Intake**
- Agents collect vendor documents (SOC reports, questionnaires, policies).
- Ensures standardized and complete submissions.

### **AI-Powered Risk Assessments**
- Google Cloud Document AI extracts data from uploaded documents.
- Vertex AI models validate security controls and identify compliance gaps.
- Cross-comparison across SIG, CAIQ, GDPR, privacy, and legal requirements.

### **Contract Intelligence**
- Automated clause extraction (SLAs, data retention, indemnification, termination).
- Risk scoring and deviation detection.
- Obligation tracking across vendor contracts.

### **Continuous Vendor Monitoring**
- Aggregates internal operational signals and external breach feeds.
- Detects emerging risks in real time and notifies stakeholders.
